OnePlace is an intuitive`point and click` application that saves and retrieves your files and documents instantly, whilst saving you valuable time in both activities.

If you'd like to know more, and have a look inside, Contact-Us and we can share our 'Guest Account' with you.

Guest Account(s) are limited, so you may have to wait for one session to end prior to being able to Sign-In.

Each and every Sign-in we refresh the guest account, so the Sign-In may take longer than usual as we prepare the account.
